Audio Visualizer/Multi room audio player
Attempt at making a music player to fit my needs and some extra. I always felt a bit disappointed with how youtube handles playlists and wish they ultimately allowed a bit more flexibility when searching within them. Thats where the idea originates from. Now with that being said, this does a lot more than just introducing a new library system.
What this achieves is a multi room music player that will work on any device with a local internet connection and a somewhat modern browser.

The music player can then be controlled from the browser of said device and new songs can be queued, played, volume controls, seeking, pausing, infinite mode, repeat mode, discover mode, queues, shutdown timer, different visualizations, realtime synchronization with other clients and much more.

The main setup concept is essentially to setup the audio nodes with whatever speakers the location has and setup the interface webhost on a local server, so it can be accessed from clients wishing to control, this could for example be a pc or a smartphone.

RTBB - Real-time Bulletin Board

RTBB is a discussion board solution made with an emphasis on reactivity, built using NestJS and Vue, it provides an almost instant feedback UI experience as well as a smooth browsing experience.
RTBB is currently in development and has not yet been published, more on that soon.

Discord Audio Bot - SlumpMaster

Music bot for Discord with emphasis on high performance and stability. Supports spotify at this moment, with plans for youtube/soundcloud. Comes with a queue system, search feature which makes it easy to find a song and play it, without having to copy paste or leave discord. Attempts to find lyrics for the track and gives a url conveniently. Overall an interactive responsive bot with emphasis on only the essentials.
